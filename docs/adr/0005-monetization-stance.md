# ADR-0005: Monetization stance

- Status: Accepted
- Date: 2026-05-19

## Context

We need recurring revenue to cover AI inference + infra without compromising RP integrity. Pay-to-win destroys trust in RP communities.

## Decision

Phase 1 monetization:

1. **VIP subscription** (Stripe, monthly).
   - Queue priority on full server.
   - Cosmetic vehicle skins.
   - Higher inventory weight cap (modest, not gameplay-defining).
   - Custom phone wallpaper.
2. **Founder packages** (one-time).
   - Custom plate.
   - Early server access during closed beta.
3. **Business ownership** — sold in-game with **in-game currency only**. Real-money business purchase is forbidden.

Forbidden:

- Selling guns, drugs, ammo, vehicles with gameplay advantage.
- Selling reputation, gang slots, faction roles.
- Selling AI tokens, story arcs, witness statements.
- Selling police priority.

Future (Phase 2+):

- AI middleware licensing — separate ADR before launch.

## Consequences

Positive:
- Trust intact. Community can defend our model publicly.
- Cosmetic + quality-of-life is enough at typical RP server scales.

Negative:
- Lower ARPU than P2W servers.
- Requires non-monetary excellence to retain — content is the moat.
